Construx Helm

The Smart Safety Helmet integrates advanced sensing technologies for proactive worker protection and emergency response. It enhances worker safety in hazardous environments through real-time data processing.

Features

  • Radar and ultrasonic sensors
  • Accelerometer and MEMS technology
  • Heartbeat monitoring
  • Obstacle classification and alerting system

Safety Protocols

The helmet issues alerts based on OSHA recommendations:

  • 10-15m for light objects
  • 20-30m for heavy objects

Alerts are delivered through visual, auditory, or vibrational signals to ensure timely warnings and reduce accident risks.
